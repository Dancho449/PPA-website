Palace of Priests Assembly is a 21st century - compliant Church established to impact the world Spiritually, and empower its members, partners and friends to conquer the seven Mountains of influence, media, government, education, economy, family, religion, and celebration (arts and entertainment), identified as the contemporary keys to taking a nation for the kingdom of God.

We believe in the purity of God's Word; we preach the Word, undiluted with no apologies; our commitment is to preserve the sanctity and purity of the Word till Jesus comes in His Glory. We are practical, we are real, we populate God's Kingdom through evangelism; we do not leave converts and members hanging and aimless, we hand-hold them by the Power of the Holy Spirit to live a triumphant Christian life and to ultimately make Heaven.

We deploy a life cycle approach that makes the Church relevant for everyone from cradle to glory. PPA is framed by the Spiritual fact and understanding that every believer is a royal priest in accordance with Exo 19:6, Rev 5:10, Rev 1:6, and 1 Peter 2:9.

Founded in 2016 by Pastor (Dr) Otive Igbuzor, PPA has a unique calling to empower people to live as Kings and Priests in this end time.

  • OUR VISION

    A world where children of God reign as Kings and Priests (Rev 5:10).

  • OUR MISSION

    To empower people to live as Kings and Priests in this end time (Rev 1:6).

  • OUR VALUES

    Our values are tacked in the memory hack/mnemonic, LERID.

Our Values

  • Leadership: requiring us to build leaders because He made us in his own image and called us to lead. We build leaders that will impact this generation and the next generation.
  • Empowerment: requiring us to equip the saints in all aspects of life to live as Kings and Priests that bring honour to God and make disciples in all nations.
  • Royalty: requiring us to live as Kings and Priests in accordance with God's design as a chosen and peculiar people.
  • Integrity: requiring us to demonstrate faith and action as children of the Most High.
  • Discipleship: requiring us to make disciples in all nations with solid biblical foundations who are then able to disciple and empower others.
